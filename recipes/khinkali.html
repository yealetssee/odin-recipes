Khinkali

Traditional Georgian Recipe

Khinkali (Georgian: ხინკალი) is a very popular Georgian dumpling made of twisted knobs of dough, stuffed with meat and spices. It is considered to be one of the national dishes of Georgia.

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Mix of ground beef and pork
  • Salt and red pepper
  • Caraway seeds
  • Water and salt for the cooking pot

Steps for cooking

  1. Add 450 ml of warm water.
  2. Mix the ingredients from the middle of the bowl until all of the flour is mixed.
  3. The dough should be formed into a ball.
  4. Divide the dough into two pieces.
