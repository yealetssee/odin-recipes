Gnocchi

Traditional Recipe

Homemade Potato Gnocchi are soft and tender and way better than store-bought. Gnocchi is also freezer-friendly. This recipe makes a generous batch, so you can freeze some for another meal.

Ingredients

  • Russet Potatoes
  • Egg
  • Flour
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps

  1. Prepare Potatoes
  2. Make Dough
  3. Knead Dough
  4. Divide the dough
  5. Cook Gnocchi
